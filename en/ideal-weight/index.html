Safe Weight Loss Pace

  • Safe: 0.5 kg/week or less
  • Caution: 0.5-1.0 kg/week
  • Dangerous: Over 1.0 kg/week (risk of muscle loss and rebound)

FAQ

Q. What is a safe weekly weight loss rate?

A. Generally, 0.5 kg/week or less is considered safe. Over 1 kg/week increases risk of muscle loss and rebound.

Q. What is the difference between standard and cosmetic weight?

A. BMI 22 (standard weight) is medically the lowest health risk. BMI 20 (cosmetic weight) is a slimmer appearance. Below BMI 18.5 is underweight with health risks.
