How to Use the Age Calculator

The Age Calculator lets you check various information at once just by entering your date of birth. Useful for filling out resumes, official procedures, and general reference.

Information Displayed

Age: Displayed as "X years Y months Z days". This tool uses the internationally standard method of counting age from 0 at birth.

Japanese Era: Converts to the corresponding Japanese era (Reiwa, Heisei, Showa, Taisho). The conversion is based on the exact start date of each era.

Chinese Zodiac: Shows the Chinese zodiac animal for your birth year. There are 12 animals in the cycle: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Boar.

Zodiac Sign: Shows your Western astrological zodiac sign based on your birthday.

Next Birthday: Shows the number of days until your next birthday.

Days Since Birth: Shows how many days have passed since you were born.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. What is the difference between full age and counting age?

A. Full age counts from 0 at birth and adds 1 on each birthday. Counting age (used in some East Asian traditions) starts at 1 at birth and adds 1 on New Year's Day. This tool uses the internationally standard full age calculation.

Q. Does this tool support Japanese eras beyond Reiwa, Heisei, and Showa?

A. This tool supports Japanese eras from Taisho onward (Taisho, Showa, Heisei, Reiwa). For dates before Taisho, only the Western calendar year is displayed.

Q. What happens for people born on February 29?

A. For people born on February 29 (leap year), in non-leap years the tool uses March 1 as the birthday for calculation purposes.
