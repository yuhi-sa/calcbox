Sleep Cycle Calculator

Calculate optimal bedtime or wake-up time based on 90-minute sleep cycles for better rest.

How It Works

Sleep occurs in 90-minute cycles. Waking at the end of a cycle helps you feel refreshed. This tool calculates optimal times based on these cycles.

Sleep Cycle Facts

  • One cycle: ~90 minutes
  • Recommended: 5-6 cycles (7.5-9 hours)
  • Minimum: 4 cycles (6 hours)
  • Average time to fall asleep: ~14 minutes

FAQ

Q. Why 90-minute cycles?

A. Sleep alternates between light sleep, deep sleep, and REM sleep in approximately 90-minute cycles. Waking during light sleep between cycles feels most natural.

Q. How much sleep do I need?

A. Most adults need 7-9 hours (5-6 cycles). Individual needs vary, but consistently getting less than 6 hours increases health risks.
