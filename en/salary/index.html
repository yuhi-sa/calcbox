How to Use the Salary Calculator

This tool estimates take-home pay from gross salary based on Japan's tax and social insurance system. Enter your gross salary (annual or monthly), age, and number of dependents. Note: This is a Japan-specific calculator using Japanese tax brackets and social insurance rates.

What is deducted?

  • Health Insurance: Approximately 5% of gross salary
  • Employee Pension: Approximately 9.15% of gross salary
  • Employment Insurance: Approximately 0.6% of gross salary
  • Nursing Care Insurance: Approximately 0.8% (for age 40+)
  • Income Tax: Progressive tax based on taxable income (5%-45%)
  • Resident Tax: Approximately 10% of taxable income
