How to Use the Loan Comparison Calculator

Enter multiple loan conditions and press "Compare" to calculate and display a comparison table of monthly payments, total payments, and total interest for each plan.

Formula

Calculated using the equal payment (fixed monthly) method. Monthly Payment = Loan Amount x Monthly Rate x (1 + Monthly Rate)^Total Months / ((1 + Monthly Rate)^Total Months - 1)

Tips

  • Plans A and B are required. Plan C is optional.
  • The plan with the lowest total interest is highlighted in green.
